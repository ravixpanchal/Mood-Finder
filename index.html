Audio Feature Analysis

Waveform

A waveform shows how the amplitude of the audio signal changes over time.

Spectrogram

A spectrogram visualizes the frequencies of a given signal as it varies with time.

MFCC (Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients)

MFCCs represent the short-term power spectrum of a sound. They are highly useful for speech processing.

Zero Crossing Rate (ZCR)

The rate at which the signal goes from positive to zero to negative or vice versa.

How MoodFinder Works

01
🎙️
Audio Input

Upload your audio file in any common format. The system accepts WAV, MP3, OGG, and more up to 25 MB.

02
🌊
Signal Processing

The audio undergoes preprocessing — framing, windowing, and FFT-based spectral analysis.

03
📐
Feature Extraction

MFCC, Chroma, ZCR, Spectral Centroid, and Mel-frequency features are extracted from the signal.

04
🤖
ML Classification

A trained classifier maps acoustic features to emotion categories with probability scores.
